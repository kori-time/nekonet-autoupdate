# Roadmap

## v0.3 — Production Hardening

- Stronger coordinator consensus
- Split-brain prevention
- Multi-site and multi-fleet support
- Additional Linux distribution support
- Package-manager abstraction
- Backup and snapshot hooks
- Signed coordinator communication
- Extended metrics and audit history

## Long-Term Goals

- Reliable zero-touch fleet maintenance
- Safe recovery from coordinator and storage failures
- Centralized certificate distribution
- Secure read-only monitoring integrations
- Flexible fleet growth without rebuilding the project
